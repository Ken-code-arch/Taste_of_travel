Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Ingredients

  • Lemongrass: 2 stalks
  • Thai basil: 1 cup
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves: 3 leaves
  • Shrimp: 500g

Preparation

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes
  2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves
  3. Stir in fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste
  4. Garnish with Thai basil and serve hot

Cooking Tips

  • ½ cup of fish sauce adds that intense Thai flavor
  • ½ tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup known for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves. It's a beloved part of Thai cuisine.

“The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors — sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.”
